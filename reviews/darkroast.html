Death Wish Coffee Co. Presents-

Dark Roast

Dark Roast lovers rejoice! Death Wish brings the depths of darkness to you with their iconic dark roast coffee. The one that started it all, it's no surprise that This roast was able to start a revolution. It has a great flavor profile on it's own and can be mixed with many options of creamers. My favorite way to make this one though is to make a nice cold brew, and then use International Delight's Cinnabon creamer, this gives you almost something of an ice cream tasting coffee for a very pleasant mornig wake up. When I'm not cold brewing it, I am often blending it with chocolate flavors. I will usually do a base 12oz coffee pod, with a double shot of one of my many chocolate flavored coffees made in my espresso maker. Add in a splash of International Delight's Hershey Chocolate creamer and it's a wonderful dessert coffee. With the depth of this dark roast, you won't often find me making anything less than a 12oz cup, because the flavor is strong enough to handle it. Where many other coffee flavors will begiun to wane and weaken after 10oz's, this one stands strong and loses ZERO intensity. I will admit, I am not personally a huge dark roast guy, other brands dark roasts are typically not for me, but there's something about this one here that does do the trick for me. While it's not going to be my daily cup, it does earn a spot in my cabinet on a consistent basis. Sometimes I find myself craving that ice cream-like flavor of the cold brew/cinnabon combo and there's just no other coffee I've found to replicate that flavor. Again, not my daily cup, but it does many things well and for that, I give 4/5 stars.

Price: $19.99 per 12 Oz. Bag
