Death Wish Coffee Co. Presents-

Blue And Buried

It will be no secret within minutes of browsing my site, I am a sucker for Death Wish Coffee Co. I won't deny it, they are in my opinion the best bang for your buck coffee on the market. That being said, this blend took them to new heights when I tried it for the first time. You'll come to know that I do not drink my coffee black, as despite my career in the financial industry, I have managed to conserve the presence of my soul. I do however try to sip a new coffee black for the first time I make it, even if it usually ends in a strange expression on my face. The Blue And Buried blend however left me more intrigued than any blend I had sipped black before. Still, I like to enjoy this with a splash of either International Delight's French Vanilla, or Planet Oat's Coffee Cake Oat Creamer. Either of these will leave you with the sensation of enjoying a fresh blueberry muffin from your favorite bakery. Blue And Buried is perfect for morning, noon and night(assuming you are like me and can down a cup of coffee before bed to unwind). Earning the rights of Quid's Favorite for the forseeable future, this is the top rated blend on the site with a rating of 5.5/5 stars.
