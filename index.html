Welcome To My Cabinet!

I am the above-mentioned 'Quid', and this is my coffee cabinet. Here you will find my honest reviews on the various kinds of coffee I have tried, including the combinations of coffee/creamer I have used! One thing I find is missing in many reviews is the reviewer's suggestion for appropriate creamer flavors. In addition to reviews, I will keep a constant ranking for my top picks, coffees that stand out to me as must-keeps in any coffee collection. I do have some brand preferences which I have established through trial and experimentation of my own coffee drinking journey, I am not paid or sponsored(yet?) by any of the brand names I list or lean towards. I hope you will find my reviews helpful or entertaining, and I look forward to sharing our coffee drinking time together!
